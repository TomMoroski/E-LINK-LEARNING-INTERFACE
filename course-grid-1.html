COURSE UNITS 1 - 6

UNIT 1

This Unit should serve as a refresher. This lesson reviews material covered from last years as well as the building blocks to lay our foundation.

UNIT 2

This module builds an understanding of Real Numbers, Scientific Notation and Proportional Relationships.

UNIT 3

This module builds an understanding of Nonproportional Relationships and Writing Linear Equations.

UNIT 4

This module builds an understanding of Functions, Solving Linear Equations and Solving Systems of Linear Equations.

UNIT 5

This Module builds an understanding of Transformations and Congruence. We also go over Transformations and Similarity.

UNIT 6

This module reviews Angle Relationships in Parallel Lines and Triangles. We will finish with the Pythagorean Theorem.
